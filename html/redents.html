RODENTS

Rodents can make great pets for those who are looking for a smaller, low-maintenance pet.
Popular rodent pets include hamsters, guinea pigs, and rats, each with their own unique
personalities and characteristics. These animals are typically easy to care for, requiring
minimal space and simple diets.


They are also quiet animals, making them great pets for apartments or small living spaces.
Additionally, rodents can be great for teaching chil- dren about responsibility and empathy,
as they require daily care and interaction. For those who may be allergic to dogs or cats,
rodents can be a great alternative pet. Overall, while they may not be the right fit for everyone,
rodents can make great pets for those who are looking for a smaller, low-maintenance animal
to provide companionship and entertainment.
